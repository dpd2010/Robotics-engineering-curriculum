## Mission Objective

Use variables to represent engineering quantities such as distance, time, speed, and voltage.

## Why This Matters

Robotics software constantly turns physical measurements into numbers. Clear variables and units reduce mistakes when software controls hardware.

## Theory

- Variables should describe what they store.
- Units must be visible in names, comments, or output.

## Coding

- Write a speed calculator.
- Print readable output with labels and units.

## Hardware

- Identify which future sensors could provide distance or voltage readings.

## Challenge

Calculate speed from three distance/time examples and label the output clearly.

## Reflection

Where could a units mistake become dangerous in a robot?
